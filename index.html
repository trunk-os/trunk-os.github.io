Powerful Features

Automated USB Install

Use any old hardware. Boot from USB and Trunk automatically configures itself and becomes available on your network at http://trunk.local

Smart Packaging System

Interactive package system asks questions and automatically configures and launches services tailored to your needs.

Automatic UPnP

Built-in UPnP support automatically forwards ports on your router. Seamless network configuration included in packaging.

Object Storage

Coming soon: Sync your phone, edit documents, and manage your own private, local AI. Complete control over your data.

Privacy First

Your data stays in your closet. No third-party cloud providers, no surveillance, no data mining. True digital independence.

Open Source

Fully open source and community-driven. Inspect the code, contribute, and help shape the future of personal cloud computing.
